About the Club

Who We Are

Founded in 2024 in partnership with Carnegie Mellon’s Quantum Computing and Information Technologies (QCiT) Center, our club brings together students from diverse backgrounds—physicists, computer scientists, engineers, and the curious alike—united by a passion for quantum computing.

At Carnegie Mellon Quantum Computing (QC @ CMU), we’re the first and only student-led organization dedicated to fostering a vibrant quantum community and advancing quantum literacy and research on campus.

What We Do

  • Host hands-on workshops and guest speaker events
  • Lead research and development projects
  • Collaborate with faculty and industry experts
  • Compete in quantum hackathons and competitions
